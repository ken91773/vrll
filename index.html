What is VRLL?

Not a game - A realistic vertical reference longline training aid

The ship, the line, and the load share a custom physics model: real tension, aerodynamic drag, and wind as true airspeed, so you feel authentic pendulum swing, load oscillation, and the parallax motion that makes vertical reference work. At its center is a unique cyclic guide, designed to compensate for the sensory cues a simulator cannot fully recreate — seat-of-the-pants feel, vestibular motion, and peripheral depth — so stick inputs stay precise when the visual picture alone is not enough. Layer on wind and load-cell cues, instruments, verbal coaching and structured lessons — in a world made for precise hover and load control.

Who it's for & why it helps

Built by a VRLL pilot for helicopter pilots (and experienced heli-simmers)

VRLL is supplemental rehearsal for pilots training toward — or already working in — vertical-reference external-load operations, and for anyone who wants to understand what longline flying really demands. Built for helicopter pilots by an experienced VRLL pilot. Also can be used by heli-simmers who want the ultimate in realism.

  • Utility & longline pilots rehearsing hover coordination and load placement between real flights.
  • Pilots in training building the vertical-reference sight picture and muscle memory.
  • Experienced simulator pilots ready to move beyond basic helicopter control and practice advanced real-world piloting concepts including vertical-reference technique, hover precision, swing management, and load placement.
  • Load & weight feel — swap between different load weights and line lengths of 50, 100, and 150 feet.
  • Hyper-realistic physics — an ultra-realistic pendulum physics model that acts and reacts to helicopter movements, unmatched by any other flight simulator.
  • Practical test practice — rehearse longline and short-haul practical tests aligned with interagency government standards.
  • Selectable stability aids — hover hold, hover SAS, altitude hold, and auto-hover to build up gradually.
  • Guided Practice & Free Play modes so you can drill a task or just fly.
  • Innovate Cyclic Guide — a game changer. Compensates for the loss of sensory perceptions in sims by providing small corrective cues.
